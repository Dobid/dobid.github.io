I am a research scientist currently working on autonomous driving at Akkodis Research.

I recently graduated from a PhD in Robotics from Paris-Saclay University, completed through an industrial collaboration between [Akkodis Research](https://www.akkodis.com/en/about/innovation) and the [ONERA SAPIA lab](https://www.onera.fr/fr/dtis-unites-de-recherche#sapia).

My doctoral research focused on leveraging Reinforcement Learning (RL) to learn how to fly a Fixed Wing Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (FW-UAV) under wind disturbances. From the skies to the road, I currently shifted my focus to the challenges of autonomous driving and I seek to transfer my experience in RL control to complex automotive environments.

My research interests are: robotics (aerial, wheeled, legged...), autonomy (through learning or classical control theory) and deep learning.
