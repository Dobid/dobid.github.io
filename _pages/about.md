Hello, I am a research scientist currently working on autonomous driving at Akkodis Research. I recently graduated from a PhD in Robotics from Paris-Saclay University, which I conducted as a part of a industrial collaboration between Akkodis Research and the [ONERA SAPIA lab](https://www.onera.fr/fr/dtis-unites-de-recherche#sapia). My PhD work focused on learning how to fly a fixed-wing UAV with Reinforcement Learning under wind disturbances, and I am currently porting my knowledge about automously controlling UAVs to autonomous driving. My research interests are: robotics (aerial, wheeled, legged...), autonomy (through learning or classical control theory) and learning methods (RL, world models).
